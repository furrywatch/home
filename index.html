Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Aura app work?
  • The Aura app connects the Sentinel camera, Guardian collar, and Sanctuary crate through a secure cloud network.
  • Collects real-time health, GPS, and environmental data, all visible and manageable through one unified mobile or web app.
  • For urgent situations, the system supports emergency intervention through the collar’s vibration or correction function, giving owners an added layer of control when immediate attention is needed.
What’s the difference between this system and traditional pet trackers?
  • Unlike standalone GPS trackers or basic health devices, the Aura app is designed for both awareness and action.
  • Combines health, GPS, behavior, and environmental monitoring into one connected system, enhanced by predictive AI that identifies unusual patterns.
  • Enables real-time response through remote vibration and correction, allowing owners to intervene instantly rather than react after the fact.
  • With a fully integrated smart crate, centralized app, and continuous monitoring, Aura moves beyond tracking into intelligent, responsive pet care.
Is the system safe for pets?
  • Safety is built into the system from the ground up.
  • All sensors are non-invasive.
  • The smart crate is designed with: fail-safe gate locking, rounded edges, smart ventilation, overheat protection, and reliable wireless communication.
  • The system also includes backup power and network redundancy where applicable, so core functionality remains available even during interruptions.
  • Our goal is to give owners confidence while keeping pet safety at the center of every design choice.
What’s the range of the GPS?
  • No fixed range limit. As long as the collar has LTE connectivity, you can track your pet’s location from anywhere in the world through the app.
Is this system modular, and why is there a subscription?
  • The Aura app is modular, so you can choose the devices and features that best fit your pet’s needs and your lifestyle. Whether you want just the collar, the crate, or the full connected setup, the system is designed to adapt. The subscription helps cover ongoing services such as LTE network access, cloud connectivity.
